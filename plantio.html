Plant.Io

Light

This indicates the ambient light near or on the plant. Depending on the plant, time of year, or temperature the neccessary light levels may change.

Water

This shows the information on the amount of moisture or humidity around the plant and in the soil it's planted in.

Happiness

This shows some information regarding the general level of "happiness" of your plant, based on multiple data inputs gathered from the sensors around your plant and your interaction with it.
